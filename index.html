About the Book

You were never lost.
You were waiting to be found.

Soul Line is a journey into the intersection of ancestral memory, spiritual identity, and DNA — the invisible thread that connects who you are today to every soul that came before you.

This book doesn't ask you to believe anything. It asks you to look closer — at the patterns in your family, the recurring themes in your life, the unexplained knowing you've always carried — and find the lineage behind it all.

Whether you're at the beginning of your awakening or deep in it, Soul Line gives you language for what you've already been feeling.
